Kurt Bryan Salvador

Pen Name: DreamSketcher

Hi there, my name is Kurt Bryan Salvador. I'm a student who is inspired by the art of minimalism. My artistic canvas is painted with a combination of words and random drawings. In my pursuit of essence, I aim to use simplicity to communicate the deepness of ideas and experiences. Come along on this passionate trip with me, where words and strokes have infinite meaning and simplicity speaks loudly.
